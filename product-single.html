    Flexible E-Commerce Template

    Shopper is a beautiful, simple and flexible e-commerce template built on top of Bootstrap. It includes dozens of components and pages you can use “as is” or customize to build exactly what you need. Built by professionals with more than five years of experience designing and developing Bootstrap templates, Shopper is a comprehensive e-commerce solution that can be used to create online stores of any complexity.

    Main Features:

    • 8 homepage variations
    • 25+ support pages including complete shop, account and blog sections
    • 10+ navigation and 4 footer variations
    • 4 mega menu dropdown variations
    • 10 modal windows for the most common use-cases
    • Detailed documentation
    • Gulp tooling to automate the workflow
    • Precompiled files for those who like to keep things simple
    • W3C valid HTML

    Design Files:

    Shopper was designed in Figma, one of the most popular design tools out there. A link to download the source Figma file is included in the package.

    Updates:

    We are constantly working on improving the template. We’ve already designed and are working on implementing even more pages and components to release in the near future. All updates are free for life!

    Support:

    Even though Shopper comes with detailed documentation, sometimes you need some additional help. We got you covered! Feel free to contact us with your questions or feature requests any time – we will get back to as quickly as possible.
